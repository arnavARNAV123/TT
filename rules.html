Summary of Table Tennis Rules

  1. Best 3 out of 5 games: The player who wins the first 3 games out of total 5 wins the match.
  2. 11 points/game: The player who wins the first 11 points wins that game - as long as there is a difference of 2 or more. If the two players reach the tally of 10-to-10, then the points are played until 2 point advantage for somebody to win that game.
  3. Alternate Serves:You and your opponent will alternate serving every two points but if the score is 10-to-10, then you and your opponent alternate serving every one point.
  4. Serve Rules: The player who is serving needs to toss the ball at least 6" above the table and bounce the ball on each table
  5. Return Rules: After serving to return the ball, you need to put the ball on the opposite side of the table.
  6. How to win a point:
    • Opponent misses the return
    • Oponent puts the ball in the net
    • Opponent puts the ball out of the table
    • Opponent misses the serve
  7. Volleys are not allowed: When you and your opponent are in a rally, if you hit the ball even before the ball hits the table, then your opponent gets a point.
  8. Honor System Applies to Dissagreements: If there is no referee and the two players are in dissagreement then the "Honor sytem" applies and the players find a way to agree to that particular situation
