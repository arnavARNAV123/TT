Fun Facts
Table-tennis is the third largest participation sport in the world after soccer and cricket.
Table tennis is known for being the smartest sport and can help mental diseases.
The longest table-tennis rally is 8 hours and 40 minutes done by father and son Peter and Daniel Ives of England. The rally lasted 32,000 total hits.
When played at an intense rate, table tennis helps you burn calories and gets you fit.
In table-tennis tournaments, an 80-year old man may play a 8-year old girl if they are in the same rating category.
Playing improves hand-eye coordination and it stimulates mental alertness, concentration and tactical strategy.
A 150-pound person can burn 272 calories by playing table tennis for an hour.
It usually takes about 10,000 hours of serious practice time (20 hours/week for 10 years) to be a world-class player or potential medalist in table-tennis at the Olympics.
